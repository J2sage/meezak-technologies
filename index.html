About Us

After launching our brands all over India and with a presence of 250+ locations across India, BrewbakesGroup Enterprises with Go Food Court is a collaborative showcase of our flagship brands of Brewbakes and Chaat ka Chaska in Xpress models and also launches Idli Xpress - unique South Indian offerings, Punjabi Adda - the heart of North Indian food and forgotten legacy recipes and Chopstick Buddies - bringing back everyone's favourite comfort food, Indo-Chinese! With an all-encompassing approach with GO FOOD, we at Brewbakes Group Enterprises aim to bring an experience like never before, to our patrons where everyone gets to have something according to their taste and preference!

Bring your family, friends or come alone, there will always be something new at the table, literally! Go Food has delicious food, for every mood!
