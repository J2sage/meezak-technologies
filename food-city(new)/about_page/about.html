Our story

Savory & Co. was born in a small kitchen with a big idea: make delicious food that feels like home, no matter where you are. What began as a pop-up dinner series quickly grew into a community of food lovers who value quality, transparency, and real taste.

We travel to meet farmers, taste every batch, and celebrate the stories behind each spice, vegetable, and grain. Our menu changes with the seasons, because food should be as alive as the people who eat it.

Today, we're proud to share our table with you — whether it's a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a special celebration.

— Elena & Marco, founders

Our mission

We exist to make honest, nourishing food accessible and joyful. Every dish we serve supports sustainable agriculture and celebrates the richness of local produce.

  • 100% traceable ingredients
  • Zero artificial additives
  • Minimal waste kitchen
  • Community first
